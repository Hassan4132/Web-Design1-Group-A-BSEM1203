About WAEC Vault

Who We Are

WAEC Vault is an educational platform designed to help students across Sierra Leone access quality learning resources for NPSE, BECE and WAEC examinations. Our platform provides revision notes, past questions, marking schemes, study guides and educational support materials to improve academic performance and examination success.


Our Mission

To make educational resources accessible to every student regardless of location and help learners prepare effectively for national examinations.


Our Vision

To become Sierra Leone's leading online educational resource platform.
